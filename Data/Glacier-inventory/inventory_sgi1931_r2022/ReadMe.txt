Swiss Glacier Inventory 1931
----------------------------

The Swiss Glacier Inventory 1931 provides areas and outlines of all glaciers in 
Switzerland referring to the mean year 1931. The data are based on terrestrial images 
and historical maps (first edition of LK25)

Attributes
----------
RivLevel3  : Subdivision of the inventory area on the basis of catchment areas of major rivers (f.e. A)
RivLevel2  : Subdivision of the inventory area on the basis of catchment areas of major tributaries (f.e. 5)
RivLevel1  : Subdivision of the inventory area on the basis of catchment areas of medium-sized tributaries (f.e. 4)
RicLevel0  : Subdivision of the inventory area on the basis of catchment areas of small tributaries (f.e. e)
InventCode : Sequential number for glacier within a specific inventory region (f.e. 24)
SGI        : Swiss Glacier Inventory Number (f.e. A54e-24)
date       : date of the terrestrial image
area_km2   : area in km2
